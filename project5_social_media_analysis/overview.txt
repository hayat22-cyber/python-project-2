Project Overview

This project analyzes social media engagement data across multiple platforms to understand which platforms and content types generate the highest engagement.






Key Insights
Instagram Reels receive the highest engagement.
Image and video posts perform better than text posts.
Higher comments strongly relate to higher likes.

Business Insights
Visual content drives more engagement
Reels and videos should be prioritized
Engagement metrics move together (likes, comments, shares).

Recommendations
Focus on short-form video content.
Increase posting frequency on Instagram.
Encourage comments to boost reach and visibility.







Conclusion
Social media performance improves significantly with visual and interactive content. Data-driven content strategy can increase engagement and reach.
